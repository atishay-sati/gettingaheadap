📝 AP Exam Breakdown

The AP U.S. History exam is 3 hours and 15 minutes long and has two sections.

Section Question Type # of Questions Time % of Score
Section I Part A: Multiple Choice 55 questions 55 minutes 40%
Part B: Short Answer 3 questions 40 minutes 20%
Section II Part A: Document-Based Question (DBQ) 1 question 60 minutes 25%
Part B: Long Essay Question (LEQ) 1 question 40 minutes 15%

💡 Study Tip

Practice SAQs, DBQs, and LEQs weekly. Focus on historical reasoning skills like causation, comparison, continuity and change, and using evidence to support a clear argument.
