Kinematics Practice Test

30 Questions – Mixed Conceptual & Calculations

1.

A particle moves along the x-axis so that its position is given by \( x(t) = 3t^3 - 2t^2 + t \) (in meters, t in seconds). What is the particle's acceleration at \( t = 2 \) s?

2.

A projectile is launched from ground level with speed \( v_0 \) at angle \( \theta \) above the horizontal. Derive an expression for the total time of flight, neglecting air resistance.

3.

A car accelerates from rest with a time-dependent acceleration \( a(t) = 6t \) m/s2. What is its velocity after 4 seconds?

4.

A ball is thrown vertically upward with initial velocity \( v_0 \). At what height (above the ground) is its speed exactly half of what it will be just before hitting the ground?

5.

A particle moves in the plane with velocity \( \vec{v}(t) = (4t)\hat{i} + (3)\hat{j} \) m/s. If it starts at the origin, what is its position vector at \( t = 2 \) s?

6.

A train moves with constant acceleration. It passes point A with speed 10 m/s and point B, 200 m further, with speed 30 m/s. What is the acceleration?

7.

A particle starts from rest and moves along a straight line with acceleration \( a(x) = 6x \) m/s2. What is its speed when it has traveled 2 m?

8.

A projectile is launched from height \( h \) above the ground with initial speed \( v_0 \) at angle \( \theta \). Derive an expression for the horizontal range.

9.

A particle moves so that its velocity is given by \( v(t) = At^2 + B \), where \( A \) and \( B \) are constants. If \( x = 0 \) at \( t = 0 \), what is its position at time \( t \)?

10.

A boat heads due east across a river with speed 5 m/s relative to the water. The river flows north at 3 m/s. What is the magnitude of the boat's velocity relative to the ground?

11.

A particle moves along the y-axis so that its velocity is given by \( v(t) = 2e^{0.5t} \) m/s. If \( y = 0 \) at \( t = 0 \), what is its position at \( t = 2 \) s?

12.

A ball is dropped from a height \( h \) above the ground. At what height is its speed exactly half of what it will be just before hitting the ground?

13.

A particle moves so that its acceleration is \( a(t) = 12t^2 \) m/s2. If its initial velocity is zero, what is its displacement after 2 seconds?

14.

A projectile is launched at speed \( v_0 \) from a cliff of height \( h \) above level ground at angle \( \theta \) above the horizontal. Which of the following best describes the time to reach the ground?

15.

A particle moves along a straight line with velocity \( v(x) = 2x \) m/s, where \( x \) is in meters. If it starts from \( x = 1 \) m at \( t = 0 \), how long does it take to reach \( x = 4 \) m?

16.

A car moves such that its velocity is \( v(t) = 10 - 2t \) m/s. At what time does it come to rest?

17.

A particle moves in a circle of radius 2 m such that its tangential acceleration is constant at 3 m/s2. If it starts from rest, what is its speed after completing one full revolution?

18.

A particle moves with velocity \( v(t) = 3t^2 - 6t + 2 \) m/s. What is its displacement from \( t = 0 \) to \( t = 3 \) s?

19.

A projectile is launched horizontally from a height \( h \) with speed \( v_0 \). How long does it take to reach the ground?

20.

A car travels 1 km at 20 m/s and then another 1 km at 40 m/s. What is its average speed for the entire trip?

21.

A particle moves along the x-axis with velocity \( v(t) = 5\cos(2t) \) m/s. If \( x = 0 \) at \( t = 0 \), what is its position at \( t = \frac{\pi}{4} \) s?

22.

A ball is thrown downward from a height \( h \) with initial speed \( v_0 \). What is its speed just before hitting the ground?

23.

A particle moves so that its acceleration is \( a(t) = -4\sin(2t) \) m/s2. If its initial velocity is zero, what is its velocity at \( t = \frac{\pi}{2} \) s?

24.

A projectile is launched at speed \( v_0 \) at angle \( \theta \) above the horizontal. What is the maximum height reached?

25.

A particle moves along a straight line so that its velocity is \( v(x) = 3x^2 \) m/s. If it starts from rest at \( x = 0 \), what is its acceleration when \( x = 2 \) m?

26.

A projectile is launched from ground level with initial speed 20 m/s at an angle of 30° above the horizontal. What is the horizontal range of the projectile?

27.

A projectile is launched with initial speed 15 m/s at an angle of 45° above the horizontal. What is the maximum height reached by the projectile?

28.

A projectile is launched at speed \( v_0 \) at angle \( \theta \) above the horizontal. What is the total horizontal distance (range) if it lands at the same height?

29.

A particle moves along the x-axis with velocity \( v(x) = kx \), where \( k > 0 \). If it starts from \( x_0 \) at \( t = 0 \), what is its position as a function of time?

30.

A car moves with velocity \( v(t) = v_0 e^{-\alpha t} \), where \( v_0 \) and \( \alpha \) are constants. What is the total distance traveled as \( t \to \infty \)?
