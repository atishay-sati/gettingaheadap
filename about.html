About Me

I'm a high school senior with a genuine passion for physics and how things work. My curiosity started at home, thanks to my mom, who always encouraged me to ask questions and explore new ideas. In high school, I've been fortunate to learn from teachers who made physics engaging and approachable, helping me see the subject as more than just equations. Their support inspired me to pursue electrical engineering in college. Outside of academics, I love playing soccer and have been on the field since I was five years old. I created this site to share what I've learned, both the challenges and the breakthroughs, in hopes that it helps other students find confidence and maybe even some excitement in physics. Thanks for visiting. I hope these resources make your journey a little easier.
