📝 AP Exam Breakdown

The AP World History: Modern exam is 3 hours and 15 minutes long and has two sections.

Section Question Type # of Questions Time % of Score
Section I Part A: Multiple Choice 55 questions 55 minutes 40%
Part B: Short Answer 3 questions 40 minutes 20%
Section II Part A: Document-Based Question (DBQ) 1 question 60 minutes (incl. 15 min reading) 25%
Part B: Long Essay Question (LEQ) 1 question 40 minutes 15%

💡 Study Tip

Practice DBQs and LEQs weekly and focus on sourcing documents (HIPP: Historical context, Intended audience, Purpose, Point of view). Explaining historical reasoning processes like causation, comparison, and continuity & change over time will sharpen both your understanding and your AP exam performance.
