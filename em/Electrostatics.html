Electrostatics

Electrostatics is around 15%–25% of your AP test. This unit focuses on electric charges, fields, and forces at rest. Understanding electric charge, Coulomb's law, and electric fields helps solve problems involving charged particles and conductors.

Now let's get into it!

Electrostatics is the study of electric charges at rest and the forces they exert on each other. In AP Physics C: Electricity & Magnetism, this unit introduces electric charge, Coulomb's law, electric fields, and electric potential. It focuses on how charged particles interact through electric forces and how electric fields are created and influenced by charge distributions. Mastery of electrostatics helps understand electric forces, field lines, and potential energy in charged systems.
